Our Services

Aluminum Scrap

Aluminum scrap can be generated during the production process of various aluminum products, such as aluminum extrusions, sheets, and coils. Other scrap types includes old or damaged car parts, such as engine blocks, wheels, and radiators, from electronic components, such as wires, cables, and circuit boards.

Copper Scrap

Copper scrap is commonly found in electrical and electronic equipment, such as wires, cables, motors, transformers, and printed circuit boards. Manufacturing facilities, such as metalworking, automotive, and aerospace industries generate significant amounts of copper scrap in the form of machining waste, offcuts, and other byproducts.

Nickel Scrap

Many industries use nickel-based alloys in their manufacturing processes, such as the aerospace, automotive, and electronics industries. This can include scrap from machining, stamping, and forming processes. Surplus military and government equipment can contain nickel-based alloys that can be recycled as scrap.

Zinc Scrap

Zinc scrap can be generated during the manufacturing process of zinc-based products, such as galvanized steel, zinc die-cast parts, and zinc alloys used in automotive and aerospace industries. Zinc scrap can be generated during the production of automobiles, such as in the manufacturing of zinc-coated steel parts.

Steel Scrap

Steel scrap comes from various sources such as mill scrap, used structural items like beams, reinforced steel & plates, plant and machineries including pipes, tubes, old vehicles, domestic goods, automotive scraps, ship building industry, railways etc.

Brass Scrap

Brass scrap comes from anchors, fasteners, cables, inserts, pneumatic parts, sanitary, electrical, LPG, and energy meter parts.

Lead Scrap

Lead scrap comes in the form of lead blocks, lead slabs from the construction industry, lead cables, weights (particularly automobile and truck wheel weights), balance lead and lead-acid batteries.

Tin Scrap

Important sources of tin scrap are used bearings, solder alloys, or bronzes
